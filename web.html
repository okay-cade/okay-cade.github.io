Illustria Frontend Redesign

As the Asset Manager of Illustria’s complete front end overhaul, I worked closely with a small team of web designers and an Art Director to ensure the final product was on brand, stayed on timeline, and was developed to perfectly coincide with our design intent.

My responsibilities during this project included:
• Design of multiple wireframes, web styles, and assets
• Direction of photography and iconography assets
• Quality assurance and consistency across pages
• File organization and management
• Ensured every element followed pre-set web styles
• Confirmed every element was on-brand
• Optimization and formatting of assets per industry standard
• Collaborated with Art Director on functionality of every element
• Worked closely with Art Director and Developer through the entire coding process to ensure the live version followed the designed mockups and development notes perfectly.

Illustria Portfolio Pages

LTo showcase Illustria’s best projects, I worked as Senior Designer with a team of 3 web designers and our Art Director to create the portfolio section of our company. These pages were essential to the new front end design, as they were to be the primary driver of sales for the company. As a team, we created a consistent method displaying projects – across different clients, different industries, and even different mediums. We used uniform layouts and mockup to display each project in a self-contained, full page collage. As as part of the Illustria front end design, my role of Asset Manager applied on all portfolio pages as well – from organization to optimization.
