Lotame eBook Graphics

Let’s be honest, an ebook about data management platforms is not the most interesting subject matter in the world. Thats where I come in — to add the design flair and reader flow that keeps people engaged. In collaboration with Lotame’s copywriters, I created numerous graphs and illustrations to better communicate some of the data-heavy topics while tying in the themes of sports and teamwork present in the overall brand campaign. By breaking down information into understandable graphics, I was able to increase Lotame’s reader engagement and elevate their ebook library.

Zozi Infographic

Armed with only a Microsoft Word document containing a few abstract statistics, I aimed to present this information in one long form infographic that Zozi planned release in a blog and email campaign. The additional guidelines of this project included working within the 6 shades of Zozi’s color palette while simultaneously further developing their illustration style. Since my natural illustration style is very vector-heavy, that proved to be no problem! This initial infographic got such a good response, that it inspired Zozi to continue the campaign by creating additional graphics on new topics, within the same illustration style and layout.
